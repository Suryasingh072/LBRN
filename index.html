Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence

Lok Bandhu Raj Narayan Inter College (LBRN) is one of Lucknow's most respected educational institutions, affiliated to UP Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad. We are committed to academic rigor, character building, and all-round student development.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted school in Lucknow, nurturing students to become responsible global citizens through innovation and values.

Our Mission

Deliver quality education that blends academic excellence with moral values, sports, and co-curricular activities for holistic growth.

"Education is not just about marks — it is about building character, instilling values, and empowering each child to face the world with confidence and compassion."

P
Dr. R. K. Sharma
Principal, LBRN School
